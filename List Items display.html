Maggie Recipe

  1. Boil 1 and Half Cups of water
  2. Break the noodle cake in half
  3. Add the maggie masala
  4. Add some veggies like:-
    • Peas
    • Carrot
    • Onion
    • Tomato
  5. Cook for 2 minutes
  6. And Our tasty maggie is ready!!
